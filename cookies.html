Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Short version: We use one tool — Vercel Analytics — to count visits anonymously. It doesn't set tracking cookies, doesn't follow you across sites, and doesn't know who you are. You can opt out below.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to remember information — like your login state, language preference, or whether you've already seen a banner. They're used by almost every website on the internet.

Some cookies are essential (the site doesn't work without them). Others are optional (analytics, advertising, personalization).

2. What we use on osyren.app

This is the full list — short and honest:

Essential storage (always active)

  • Cookie consent preference — we save your choice (Accept/Decline) in your browser's local storage so we don't show you the banner on every visit. This isn't technically a cookie, it's localStorage, and it never leaves your device.
  • Language preference — your selected language (EN/RO/IT/ES) is saved in localStorage so the site loads in your language next time.

Optional analytics (only if you accept)

  • Vercel Web Analytics — counts page views, visitor countries, devices (mobile/desktop), and operating systems. It does not use cookies. Visitors are identified by an anonymous hash of the request, automatically discarded after 24 hours. No personal information is collected, no cross-site tracking happens.

If you click "Decline" on our banner, Vercel Analytics is never loaded. Period. We respect your choice.

3. What we do NOT use

  • No advertising cookies — we don't run ads.
  • No social media tracking pixels — no Facebook Pixel, no LinkedIn Insight Tag.
  • No Google Analytics — we chose Vercel's privacy-friendly alternative instead.
  • No fingerprinting — we don't try to identify you by your device's unique characteristics.
  • No third-party trackers — only Vercel Analytics, only if you accept.
